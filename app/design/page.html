Usage Guidelines

  • Primary Brand: Use sky blue to teal gradients for main navigation and headers
  • Interactive Elements: Cyan to emerald for buttons and clickable components
  • Success States: Emerald to lime for completed actions and positive feedback
  • Backgrounds: Soft white and translucent overlays for content areas
  • Accents: Bright lime for growth metrics, new features, and call-to-action elements
  • Accessibility: Ensure sufficient contrast ratios, especially with translucent elements
  • Consistency: Stick to 2-3 gradients per interface to maintain visual harmony
